# Startup-Funding-and-Outcome-Analysis

## Overview
This project analyses startup funding and outcome data to investigate the key factors that influence business success or failure. The aim is to provide insights into investment risk by examining financial, operational, and market-related variables.

## Objectives
- Analyse the relationship between funding and startup outcomes (IPO, Acquisition, Failure)
- Identify key risk factors affecting startup success
- Evaluate the impact of founder experience, sector, and investor type
- Provide data-driven insights relevant to investment decision-making

## Dataset
The dataset was sourced from Kaggle and includes information on:
- Funding rounds
- Founder experience and background
- Team size
- Market size
- Product traction (users)
- Burn rate and revenue
- Investor type and sector
- Final outcome (IPO, Acquisition, Failure)
